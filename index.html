Wellesley & Simmons Hackathon

April 17-19th 2015

What is this?

This is a hackathon for awesome women in the Boston area who like to make cool things. The main goal is to encourage learning new technologies and to provide networking opportunities between students and industry professionals by having integrated teams. The theme will be open to encourage building webapps, mobile apps, or software development tools. In order to effectively foster a unique learning opportunity and a networking oriented experience, teams of 4-5 will be composed of a Wellesley student host (to provide living accommodations), students from other colleges, and a team mentor. This team construction will create an equal playing field for each team and allow intercollegiate networking.

Who is this for?

This event is focused on creating a space for undergraduates women in the New England area to learn, network, and build. We are focused on supporting women in tech, and on creating an environment that is beginner-friendly.
